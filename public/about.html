About

This Burger Fest is not one to be missed. The line up is phenomenal - bringing back some Birminghams firm favourites, as well as welcoming some incredible out of town burger masters too. Slinging burgers into your mouth are; Patty Freaks, Original Patty Men, Nanny Bills, Andy Low 'N' Slow, Flying Cows, Libertine Burger, Homeboys, Baked in Brick and Yardbirds. With sides, desserts and hot beverages from Disco Fries, Street Chef, The Bournville Waffle Company and Stop the Press. You should expect serious burger specials from some of the best. Make sure you save some room for dessert as waffle legends, The Bournville Waffle Co, will be with us on the day too.

There will be everything from cocktails, beer, soft drinks available as well as coffee & tea from Stop the Press that will quench your thirst. With burgers, drinks and live bands and DJ sets throughout the day, does it get much better than that? Tickets will be £5 (£3.50 early birds) and available through Design My Night but make sure you get them quickly as if you have ever been to one of the Seasonal Markets events, expect a complete sell-out. Children under 12s get in for free.
